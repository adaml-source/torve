Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Introduction

Torve ("the App," "we," "our") is a media companion application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App. By using Torve, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Torve is designed with a local-first approach — your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to connect to third-party services.

Information We Do Not Collect

Torve does not operate any backend servers. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • Track your viewing habits or watch history on our servers
  • Collect analytics or usage telemetry
  • Serve advertisements or share data with advertisers
  • Sell, rent, or trade any user information to third parties

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Watch history and playback progress
  • Extension configurations and playlist URLs
  • Cached metadata (titles, descriptions, artwork) for performance
  • Authentication tokens for services you choose to connect

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any server operated by us. You can clear all local data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services You May Connect

Torve allows you to optionally connect to third-party services to enhance your experience. When you choose to connect a service, your interactions with that service are governed by its own privacy policy and terms. We do not control, endorse, or assume responsibility for the privacy practices of any third-party service.

Categories of third-party services you may connect include:

Media Tracking Services

If you connect a tracking account (such as Trakt.tv or SIMKL), your watch activity may be shared with that service according to its privacy policy.

Cloud Media Services

If you connect a cloud media service, the App communicates with that provider's API using credentials you provide. Your API key or access token is stored locally on your device and transmitted only to the respective provider's servers.

AI Search Providers

If you enable AI-powered search, your search queries are sent to the AI provider you select (such as Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Perplexity, or DeepSeek). These queries may contain the text you type into the search field. No personal information, watch history, or account data is included in these queries. Your AI provider API key is stored locally on your device. Please review the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider for details on how they handle query data.

Media Server Integration

If you connect a personal media server (such as Jellyfin or Plex), the App communicates directly with your server using credentials you provide. This communication occurs between your device and your server only — no data passes through our systems.

Metadata Providers

The App uses TMDB (The Movie Database) and, if configured, MDBList to retrieve content metadata such as titles, descriptions, ratings, and artwork. These requests contain only content identifiers (such as movie or show IDs) and do not include any personal information.

Authentication & Credentials

API keys, access tokens, and other credentials you provide are stored locally on your device. They are transmitted only to the specific service they belong to and are never included in backup exports, diagnostic reports, or any communication with our systems.

Log Data

The App does not collect crash logs or error reports remotely. Diagnostic information is available within the App for your own troubleshooting purposes and is only shared if you explicitly choose to copy or send it.

Cookies

The App does not use cookies. Third-party services you connect to may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Torve is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through a connected third-party service, please contact us so we can advise on appropriate steps.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored on your device, including storing authentication credentials in secure device storage where available. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of locally stored data. We recommend using device-level security features such as screen lock and encryption.

Your Rights

Because Torve does not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. All data is under your control on your device. You can:

  • View all stored settings and preferences within the App
  • Disconnect any third-party service at any time
  • Export or delete your local data at any time
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data

International Users

When you connect to third-party services, your data may be transferred to servers located outside your country of residence. These transfers are initiated by you and governed by the privacy policies of the respective third-party services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be made available within the App and on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
