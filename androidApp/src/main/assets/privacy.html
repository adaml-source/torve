Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Overview

Torve is free software under AGPL-3.0-or-later. Local functionality can be used without a paid plan where supported. An account is required for cross-device sync, device linking, account-backed watch state, preferences, data controls, and other account-scoped cloud services.

Torve does not sell personal data. Payment, entitlement, purchase history, and donation status do not determine product access.

Data Torve May Process

  • Account email, display name, password hash, authentication tokens, verification and reset-token records.
  • Registered devices, installation identifiers, platform, app version, device names, device status, and pairing state.
  • Account-backed settings, preferences, watch state, favorites, playlist metadata, and integration metadata where sync is enabled.
  • Encrypted account-mode integration credentials when you choose to save credentials to your Torve account.
  • Diagnostics, crash reports, request metadata, service logs, and support communications.
  • Historical payment or subscription records only where retained for refund, accounting, dispute, fraud-prevention, reconciliation, or legal obligations.

Local Data

Torve stores local app preferences, cache data, playback state, extension configuration, playlist data, and credentials on your device. Some integrations can be kept device-only and are not synced through Torve account services.

Third-Party Services And Processors

Torve may use infrastructure, email delivery, Google Play services, Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics in Google Play builds, app stores, and support providers as needed to operate the app and account services. Third-party integrations you configure, such as metadata providers, tracking services, AI providers, personal media servers, and cloud media providers, are governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Why Data Is Processed

Torve processes data to authenticate accounts, provide sync, link and manage devices, protect ownership boundaries, operate account deletion, send service emails, troubleshoot reliability, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Retention

Account-backed data is retained while your account is active unless deleted earlier through app controls or account deletion. Diagnostic and server logs are retained only as long as operationally needed. Historical payment records may be retained only for refund, accounting, dispute, fraud-prevention, reconciliation, or legal obligations.

Account Deletion

You can delete your Torve account in the app where supported or through https://torve.app/account-deletion.html. Account deletion removes account profile data and account-backed data such as registered devices, pairings, settings, synced preferences, watch state, favorites, and account-mode integration records. Narrow records may be retained where required for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, backups, disputes, historical refund reconciliation, or operational integrity.

Your Choices

  • Use local functionality without a paid plan where supported.
  • Keep supported integrations device-only instead of account-synced.
  • Disconnect integrations and clear local app data from the app or Android settings.
  • Request account deletion if you no longer want Torve account-backed data retained.

For privacy-related questions, contact privacy@torve.app.
