Torve - Store Metadata Cleanup Summary
======================================
Date: 2026-06-15

Torve is free software. There are no subscriptions, no paid tiers, no premium features, and no purchase required. Donations are optional and never unlock features.

Repository code and docs have moved away from active paid-product positioning, but external store metadata still requires manual cleanup outside this repository.

Required external cleanup:

1. Google Play mobile
   - Remove in-app products and subscriptions.
   - Remove premium, paywall, subscription, purchase, and paid-tier listing copy.
   - Remove paywall screenshots.
   - Verify donation links are hidden unless policy-reviewed.
   - Review Firebase key restrictions.

2. Google TV / Android TV
   - Remove TV in-app products and subscriptions.
   - Remove premium and purchase TV listing copy.
   - Remove paywall screenshots.
   - Verify donation links are hidden by default.

3. Amazon Appstore / Fire TV
   - Remove Amazon IAP products.
   - Remove subscription, premium, lifetime, checkout, and restore wording.
   - Verify donation links are hidden by default.

4. iOS App Store
   - Remove IAP and subscription products.
   - Remove paywall screenshots.
   - Remove premium and purchase copy.
   - Verify donation links are hidden by default unless policy-reviewed.

5. Desktop
   - Ensure packaging embeds AGPL-3.0-or-later license text or a pointer to the top-level LICENSE.
   - Keep signing credentials and appcast/admin secrets outside the repository.

Publication note:

This private repository should not be made public directly. The public release should be created from a fresh sanitized source export after final audit.
