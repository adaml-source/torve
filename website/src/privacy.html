Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026. Last updated: June 21, 2026.

Torve is free software. This policy explains data handling for the website, apps, and account-backed services. Payment, purchase history, transaction history, and donation status do not determine product access.

Data Torve may process

  • Account identity data such as email, display name, account identifiers, authentication status, and verification state.
  • Device and app data such as device type, device identifiers, app version, installation identifiers, paired-device information, device linking state, and diagnostics.
  • Account-backed settings, preferences, watch state, saved items, source configuration metadata, and sync data where supported.
  • Support communications and legal/privacy requests you send to Torve.
  • Basic website and server logs needed for security and reliability.

Why data is processed

Torve processes data to authenticate accounts, provide sync, link devices, protect ownership boundaries, operate account deletion, send service emails, prevent abuse, troubleshoot reliability, and comply with legal obligations.

Diagnostics and telemetry

Torve may process diagnostic information, crash reports, feature reliability events, request metadata, and service logs. Do not send credentials or sensitive third-party data in public bug reports.

Historical records

Historical purchase and transaction records may be retained where necessary for refunds, accounting, tax, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and support. They do not determine product access.

Sharing

Torve may use infrastructure, email delivery, app store, diagnostics, crash reporting, and support providers as needed to operate the service. Torve does not sell personal data.

Retention

Account data is retained while the account is active and for limited periods afterward where needed for security, backups, legal compliance, or operational integrity. Data is deleted, anonymized, or isolated when retention is no longer needed.

Your controls

You can request access, correction, export, deletion, or other privacy actions by contacting privacy@torve.app. You can delete your account from the app or from the account deletion page.
