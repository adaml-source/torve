# Google Play Listing Copy Package

## App Title

Torve

## Short Description

Free software media companion with optional account sync.

## Full Description

Torve is a free software media companion for organizing local media workflows
and account-backed sync across devices.

There are no subscriptions, no paid tiers, no premium features, and no purchase
required. Local-only functionality works without payment where supported. Sign
in is required for cross-device sync, device linking, account-backed watch
state, preferences, data controls, and other account-scoped cloud services.

Torve does not provide movies, shows, channels, playlists, streams, IPTV
service, or rights to third-party content. Use only lawful sources and services
you are allowed to access.

Donations are optional and never unlock features.

## Privacy Summary

Torve processes account data for authentication, sync, device linking,
account-backed preferences, watch state, diagnostics, support, account deletion,
and security. Torve does not sell personal data. Do not submit credentials or
sensitive user data in public bug reports.

## Account And Sync Explanation

You can use Torve locally without a paid plan where local functionality is
supported. Sign in is required for cross-device sync, device linking,
account-backed watch state and preferences, data export, account deletion, and
other authenticated account services.
