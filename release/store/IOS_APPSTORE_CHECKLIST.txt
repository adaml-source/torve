-----------
[ ] Listing states: Torve is free software.
[ ] Listing states: no subscriptions, no paid tiers, no premium features, no in-app purchases, no purchase required.
[ ] Remove IAP and subscription products from App Store Connect.
[ ] Remove paywall, premium, restore-purchase, subscription, and purchase screenshots/copy.

Testing
-------
