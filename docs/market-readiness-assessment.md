# Torve Market Readiness Assessment

Obsolete after free-software conversion. Retained for historical reference only.

This document previously assessed Torve as a paid product with premium features, founder lifetime offers, subscription pricing, and revenue-readiness scenarios. That positioning is no longer current.

Current canonical positioning:

Torve is free software. There are no subscriptions, no paid tiers, no premium features, and no purchase required. Donations are optional and never unlock features.

## Current Readiness Focus

Future readiness assessments should focus on:

- free-software license completion
- sanitized public repository preparation
- secret and archive audit
- store metadata cleanup
- privacy and diagnostics accuracy
- real-device QA
- public CI safety
- community contribution process
- optional non-gating donation placement

Do not reuse previous pricing, founder sale, premium differentiator, paid beta, or revenue-plan language for public release material.
