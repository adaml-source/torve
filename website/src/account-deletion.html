Delete your Torve account

This public page can be used from mobile, desktop, or TV browser entry. Account deletion is not subscription cancellation; Torve has no subscriptions or access tiers.

Sign in first, then type DELETE to submit account deletion through the web session.

If you cannot sign in, email support@torve.app from the address associated with your account and use the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

What is deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Login credentials and active sessions
  • Account-backed preferences and sync data
  • Linked device associations
  • Account-backed watch state and saved items where stored by Torve

Limited retention

Torve may retain narrow records only where needed for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, backups, dispute handling, historical refund reconciliation, or operational integrity.

Read the privacy policy

In-app deletion path

  1. Open Torve.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Open Account.
  5. Select Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.
